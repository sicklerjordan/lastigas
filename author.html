About IGas Usa

iGas USA, Inc. offers a full range of refrigerants. Our strength, and our mission, is to develop and bring to the market innovative solutions that will have a positive effect on a company's work and business.

Each day we strive to design creative, safe and high quality products that will: assist in lowering your cost of business, make daily work requirements easier, faster and more economical and improve working conditions and the environment.
